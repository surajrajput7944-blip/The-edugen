Science Chapter 1 Class 10 MCQ

Chemical Reactions and Equations (Hard Level)

Q1. The reaction in which a more reactive metal displaces a less reactive metal is:
A) Combination
B) Displacement
C) Decomposition
D) Neutralisation
Correct Answer: B) Displacement
Q2. Loss of electrons is called:
A) Reduction
B) Oxidation
C) Neutralisation
D) Sublimation
Correct Answer: B) Oxidation
Q3. The reaction 2AgCl → 2Ag + Cl₂ in sunlight is:
A) Thermal decomposition
B) Electrolysis
C) Photochemical decomposition
D) Neutralisation
Correct Answer: C) Photochemical decomposition
Q4. Which substance is reduced in the reaction: CuO + H₂ → Cu + H₂O?
A) CuO
B) H₂
C) Cu
D) H₂O
Correct Answer: A) CuO
Q5. Rusting of iron requires:
A) Oxygen only
B) Water only
C) Oxygen and water
D) Carbon dioxide only
Correct Answer: C) Oxygen and water
Q6. Which is an example of double displacement reaction?
A) Zn + HCl
B) AgNO₃ + NaCl
C) CaO + H₂O
D) C + O₂
Correct Answer: B) AgNO₃ + NaCl
Q7. Rancidity is caused due to:
A) Reduction
B) Oxidation of fats
C) Hydration
D) Sublimation
Correct Answer: B) Oxidation of fats
Q8. In a redox reaction:
A) Only oxidation occurs
B) Only reduction occurs
C) Both occur simultaneously
D) No change occurs
Correct Answer: C) Both occur simultaneously
Q9. Which gas turns lime water milky?
A) Oxygen
B) Nitrogen
C) Hydrogen
D) Carbon dioxide
Correct Answer: D) Carbon dioxide
Q10. The chemical name of rust is:
A) Iron oxide
B) Hydrated iron oxide
C) Iron sulphide
D) Ferric chloride
Correct Answer: B) Hydrated iron oxide
Q11. Which reaction releases heat?
A) Endothermic
B) Exothermic
C) Neutral
D) Reversible
Correct Answer: B) Exothermic
Q12. CaCO₃ → CaO + CO₂ is:
A) Combination
B) Decomposition
C) Displacement
D) Redox
Correct Answer: B) Decomposition
Q13. Which prevents corrosion?
A) Painting
B) Washing
C) Heating
D) Cooling
Correct Answer: A) Painting
Q14. A reaction forming precipitate is:
A) Combination
B) Double displacement
C) Decomposition
D) Sublimation
Correct Answer: B) Double displacement
Q15. The colour of copper sulphate solution is:
A) Green
B) Blue
C) Red
D) Yellow
Correct Answer: B) Blue
Q16. Magnesium ribbon burns with:
A) Blue flame
B) Yellow flame
C) White flame
D) No flame
Correct Answer: C) White flame
Q17. Oxidation is:
A) Gain of hydrogen
B) Loss of oxygen
C) Loss of electrons
D) Gain of electrons
Correct Answer: C) Loss of electrons
Q18. Which is a corrosion example?
A) Burning
B) Melting
C) Rusting
D) Boiling
Correct Answer: C) Rusting
Q19. Silver chloride is:
A) Black
B) White
C) Green
D) Blue
Correct Answer: B) White
Q20. Reduction involves:
A) Gain of electrons
B) Loss of electrons
C) Gain of oxygen
D) Loss of hydrogen
Correct Answer: A) Gain of electrons
Q21. Which reaction absorbs heat?
A) Endothermic
B) Exothermic
C) Neutralisation
D) Displacement
Correct Answer: A) Endothermic
Q22. Zn + CuSO₄ gives:
A) ZnSO₄ + Cu
B) CuSO₄ + Zn
C) Zn + Cu
D) No reaction
Correct Answer: A) ZnSO₄ + Cu
Q23. Which is not a chemical change?
A) Rusting
B) Burning
C) Melting ice
D) Digestion
Correct Answer: C) Melting ice
Q24. Oil and fatty foods are packed with nitrogen to prevent:
A) Reduction
B) Oxidation
C) Corrosion
D) Hydration
Correct Answer: B) Oxidation
Q25. Balancing of equation follows:
A) Law of conservation of mass
B) Law of gravity
C) Law of motion
D) Law of reflection
Correct Answer: A) Law of conservation of mass
Q26. The reaction between quicklime and water is:
A) Endothermic
B) Exothermic
C) Neutral
D) Reversible
Correct Answer: B) Exothermic
Q27. Which compound decomposes in sunlight?
A) NaCl
B) AgCl
C) HCl
D) KCl
Correct Answer: B) AgCl
Q28. In CuO + H₂ → Cu + H₂O, the reducing agent is:
A) CuO
B) H₂
C) Cu
D) H₂O
Correct Answer: B) H₂
Q29. Corrosion of copper forms:
A) CuO
B) Cu₂O

D) Rust
Correct Answer: C) Green coating
Q30. Which metal is more reactive?
A) Cu
B) Zn
C) Ag
D) Au
Correct Answer: B) Zn
Q31. A balanced equation has:
A) Equal atoms both sides
B) Equal molecules only
C) Equal mass only
D) Equal charges only

Q32. Which is a chemical change?
A) Boiling water

Correct Answer: C) Burning candle
Q33. Decomposition reaction needs:
A) Heat/light/electricity

Q34. Which is oxidation?
A) Gain of hydrogen

Q35. Which prevents rancidity?
A) Exposure to air
B) Storing in light

A) Zn + CuSO₄
B) CaO + H₂O

Q37. Iron reacts with oxygen to form:
A) FeO
B) Fe₂O₃

Q38. Which reaction forms precipitate?
A) AgNO₃ + NaCl

Correct Answer: A) AgNO₃ + NaCl
Q39. Burning of natural gas produces:
A) CO₂ + H₂O
B) CO only
C) H₂ only
D) O₂ only
Correct Answer: A) CO₂ + H₂O
Q40. Which reaction type is respiration?
A) Endothermic
B) Exothermic
C) Neutral
D) Reversible
Correct Answer: B) Exothermic
Q41. Which is example of combination reaction?
A) 2H₂ + O₂ → 2H₂O
B) CaCO₃ → CaO + CO₂
C) Zn + CuSO₄
D) AgNO₃ + NaCl
Correct Answer: A) 2H₂ + O₂ → 2H₂O
Q42. Green coating on copper is:
A) Copper oxide
B) Copper carbonate
C) Copper sulphate
D) Copper nitrate
Correct Answer: B) Copper carbonate
Q43. Which reaction is double displacement?
A) HCl + NaOH
B) Zn + HCl
C) Mg + O₂
D) CaCO₃ → CaO
Correct Answer: A) HCl + NaOH
Q44. Law of conservation of mass states:
A) Matter created
B) Matter destroyed
C) Matter neither created nor destroyed
D) Matter changes
Correct Answer: C) Matter neither created nor destroyed
Q45. Reduction is:
A) Gain of electrons
B) Loss of electrons
C) Gain of oxygen
D) Loss of hydrogen
Correct Answer: A) Gain of electrons
Q46. Which is decomposition?
A) 2KClO₃ → 2KCl + 3O₂
B) 2H₂ + O₂
C) Zn + CuSO₄
D) HCl + NaOH
Correct Answer: A) 2KClO₃ → 2KCl + 3O₂
Q47. Aluminium prevents corrosion by:
A) Painting
B) Forming oxide layer
C) Cooling
D) Washing
Correct Answer: B) Forming oxide layer
Q48. Which is example of displacement?
A) Fe + CuSO₄
B) NaCl + AgNO₃
C) CaO + H₂O
D) H₂ + O₂
Correct Answer: A) Fe + CuSO₄
Q49. Which is oxidation?
A) Addition of hydrogen
B) Removal of oxygen
C) Addition of oxygen
D) Gain of electrons
Correct Answer: C) Addition of oxygen
Q50. The white powder formed on heating limestone is:
A) CaO
B) CaCO₃
C) CaCl₂
D) CaSO₄
Correct Answer: A) CaO
